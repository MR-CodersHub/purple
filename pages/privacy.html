Privacy & Data Sovereignty

At PurpleNest, your privacy is a paramount commitment. This scroll outlines our meticulous approach to the collection, stewardship, and protection of your personal information.

Information Acquisition

We gather selectively provided information during your engagement with the House of PurpleNest, including acquisitions, account creation, or concierge subscriptions. This may encompass your appellation, electronic mail, delivery domicile, and financial particulars.

Utilization of Particulars

Your data serves to facilitate seamless transactions, bespoke communications regarding your acquisitions, and the refinement of our luxury offerings. Periodic insights into our exclusive collections may be shared, with an invitation to opt-out at your discretion.

Custodianship & Sharing

PurpleNest maintains absolute confidentiality; we do not trade your particulars with external entities. Information is shared solely with trusted partners essential to our operations, under strict non-disclosure mandates.

Digital Integrity

We employ cutting-edge cryptographic measures to ensure the sanctity of your personal data during every phase of your journey with us.
