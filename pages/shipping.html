Logistics & Returns

Premium Carriage

We offer meticulously handled shipping options to ensure your acquisitions arrive in pristine condition:

  • White-Glove Standard (3-5 business days): Complimentary for orders above $500
  • Priority Express (1-2 business days): $25.00
  • International Courier: Calculated at acquisition

All orders are dispatched from our Manhattan atelier within 24 hours.

Satisfaction Guarantee

If your acquisition does not meet your expectations, we offer a seamless return process within 30 days of receipt.

  • Complimentary return shipping for damaged or incorrect items.
  • Items must be returned in their original, unopened luxury packaging.
  • Refunds are typically credited back to the original source within 5-7 bank days.

To initiate a return portal, please contact our concierge at concierge@purplenest.com.
