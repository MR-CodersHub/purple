Client Support & FAQ

What distinguished payment methods do you facilitate?

What is the anticipated timeframe for carriage?

Our standard white-glove delivery typically arrives within 3 to 5 business days. For urgent requirements, priority express options are available at checkout.

We offer a 30-day window for returns of unused and pristine items. Our commitment is to your complete satisfaction; please consult our Shipping page for comprehensive details.

Indeed, PurpleNest delivers to a global clientele across select nations. Carriage fees and duties are meticulously calculated during the acquisition process.
